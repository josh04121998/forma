# Privacy Policy for Forma

**Last Updated: February 15, 2026**

## Overview

Forma ("we", "our", or "the app") is a fitness tracking application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally (On Your Device)
- Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
- Workout templates you create
- Profile information (age, height, weight, fitness goals)
- App preferences and settings

**This data never leaves your device unless you create an account.**

### Data Stored in the Cloud (If You Create an Account)
If you choose to create an account, the following data is synced to our secure servers:
- Email address
- Workout history
- Profile information
- Custom templates

We use **Supabase** for secure cloud storage and authentication.

### AI Coach Feature
When you use the AI Coach feature:
- Your profile data (age, weight, height, goals, experience level) is sent to our AI provider to generate personalized workout and meal plans
- We use **Groq** to process AI requests
- No personally identifiable information beyond fitness metrics is shared

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide workout tracking functionality
- To sync your data across devices (if you have an account)
- To generate personalized AI workout and meal plans
- To improve the app experience

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for purposes other than providing the app service

We may share data with:
- **Supabase** (cloud database and authentication)
- **Groq** (AI processing for Coach feature)

## Data Retention

- Local data: Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app
- Cloud data: Stored until you delete your account
- You can export or delete your data at any time

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your personal data
- Export your workout data
- Use the app without creating an account

## Children's Privacy

Forma is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication via Supabase
- No storage of payment information (handled by Apple)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
